# Story of Online Retail Company

Someone decides to sell a product. [Product Marketer Maintains Product Information](Product%20Marketer%20Maintains%20Product%20Information).
[Buyer Places Purchase Order](Buyer%20Places%20Purchase%20Order). [Marketer Maintains General Site Content](Marketer%20Maintains%20General%20Site%20Content).

When new inventory is needed [Buyer Places Purchase Order](Buyer%20Places%20Purchase%20Order).
When shipments arrive [Shipment Receiver Receives Shipment](Shipment%20Receiver%20Receives%20Shipment), then [Inventory Mover Puts Away Inventory](Inventory%20Mover%20Puts%20Away%20Inventory).

[Customer Finds Product](Customer%20Finds%20Product). [Placing Customer Adds Product to Cart](Placing%20Customer%20Adds%20Product%20to%20Cart)
and [Placing Customer Places a Sales Order - Online Checkout](Placing%20Customer%20Places%20a%20Sales%20Order%20-%20Online%20Checkout).
Alternatively [Placing Customer Places a Sales Order - Through CSR](Placing%20Customer%20Places%20a%20Sales%20Order%20-%20Through%20CSR)
or [Placing Customer Places a Sales Order - Through EBay](Placing%20Customer%20Places%20a%20Sales%20Order%20-%20Through%20EBay).
If Customer decides to, [Customer Cancels Order](Customer%20Cancels%20Order).

If desired during checkout or for other reasons, [Customer Creates and Edits Account](Customer%20Creates%20and%20Edits%20Account).
As desired [Customer Reviews Order History and Status](Customer%20Reviews%20Order%20History%20and%20Status).

Once order is ready [Company Approves Sales Order](Company%20Approves%20Sales%20Order).

When inventory is not sufficient in a pick location [Inventory Mover does Stock Move](Inventory%20Mover%20does%20Stock%20Move).
[Company Fulfills Sales Order](Company%20Fulfills%20Sales%20Order). [Accountant Sends Invoice and Receives Payment](Accountant%20Sends%20Invoice%20and%20Receives%20Payment).
Sometimes special cases come up, like [Carrier Does Not Deliver Shipment to Customer](Carrier%20Does%20Not%20Deliver%20Shipment%20to%20Customer)
and [Carrier Returns Undeliverable Shipment to Company](Carrier%20Returns%20Undeliverable%20Shipment%20to%20Company).

[Customer Returns Product](Customer%20Returns%20Product). [Company Processes Return Received](Company%20Processes%20Return%20Received).
